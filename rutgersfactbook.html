Rutgers University Fact Book

The Rutgers University Fact Book provides a comprehensive source of information about Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey. It contains historical and current data about our students, faculty, finances, facilities, and instruction.

The Fact Book is available as a PDF. If the specific data that you are looking for is not posted, contact our office.
