Disclosures: Fire Safety

An annual fire safety report includes statistics regarding the number/causes of all fires in on-campus housing facilities, number of fire injuries and deaths, and value of property damage. The report also includes information concerning each on-campus student housing fire safety system, the number of regular mandatory fire drills, fire safety policies and education programs, and plans for any needed fire safety improvements.
