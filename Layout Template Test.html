What We Do

The Office of Institutional Research and Academic Planning delivers analytical and consulting services to central, chancellor, collegiate, and administrative areas to support academic and administrative planning and evaluation. Specific activities of OIRAP include responsibility for new program development; institutional accreditation and licensing, academic program review, data and analytical support for university offices and committees; survey development, administration, and analysis; placement testing of new students; and internal, inter-institutional, state, and federal reporting.
