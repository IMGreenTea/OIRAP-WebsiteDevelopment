Disclosures: Institutional and Specialized Accreditation

Rutgers University is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education, 3624 Market Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104-2680 (267/284-5000). The Middle States Commission on Higher Education is an institutional accrediting agency recognized by the U.S. Secretary of Education and the Council for Higher Education Accreditation. That accreditation was reaffirmed in 2013 following a Periodic Review Report. The Middle States Commission on Higher Education website includes information on how to file a complaint regarding an accredited institution.

Certain programs on the Camden, Newark, and New Brunswick campuses of Rutgers are subject to specialized accreditation.
