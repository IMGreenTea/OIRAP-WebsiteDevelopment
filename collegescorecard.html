College Scorecard

The U.S. Department of Education offers the College Scorecard to provide students and families the most reliable, comprehensive, nationally comparable data on institutional outcomes. The Scorecard includes statistics on:

  • debt;
  • federal loan repayment;
  • completion rates, and:
  • post-college earnings

of alumni in an easy-to-understand format.
