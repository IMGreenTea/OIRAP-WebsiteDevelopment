Surveys

The Office of Institutional Research is actively engaged in conducting a robust survey research program that includes participation in nationally developed survey projects as well as the construction and administration of locally-developed survey instruments. The focus of this program is wide-ranging, involving many of the university's constituencies. These survey activities are part of the university's commitment to institutional assessment and effectiveness, and sustain a culture of evidence and institutional improvement. The following provides an overview of some of these efforts, with embedded links directing the reader to more detailed and specific information about each area of activity.
