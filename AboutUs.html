The Office of Institutional Research and Academic Planning (OIRAP) gathers, analyzes, and uses data to help the university community with their institutional planning, policy development, and decision-making needs. It provides the following critically important services to support Rutgers effectiveness and to respond to the needs of the entire university community and its external stakeholders.

  • Reporting
  • Assessment
  • Benchmarking
  • Planning
  • Public information services

OIRAP delivers analytical and consulting services to central, chancellor, collegiate, and administrative areas to support academic and administrative planning and evaluation. Our specific activities include the following responsibilities:

  • New program development
  • Institutional accreditation and licensing
  • Academic program review
  • Data and analytical support for university offices and committees
  • Survey development, administration, and analysis
  • Placement testing of new students
  • Internal, inter-institutional, state, and federal reporting
